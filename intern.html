Computer Science Outreach Internship

Are you looking for an internship where you can make a difference in the Computer Science Community around the world? Then you should consider applying to be a Def Hacks CS Outreach Intern. Through this internship you will work on various projects, working to give students in underrepresented communities opportunities to get more engaged in CS.

Required skills:

  • Full Stack Development
  • 2+ years of Computer Science
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Recommended skills:

  • Curriculum Development
  • Outreach

Submit your application through the button below and we will contact you if we feel you are a good fit for our program.
